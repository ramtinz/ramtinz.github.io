## 🔬 Research & Projects

### Key Projects
- **Explainable AI for Biomedical Research**
  - Developed the `ExplaineR` CRAN package for interpretable machine learning, utilizing SHAP methods to aid medical research and model explainability.
  - Created Python frameworks to streamline ML workflows for classification, regression, and survival analyses in biomedical contexts.

- **Predictive Modeling in Healthcare**
  - Collaborated on predictive modeling for national and international cohort studies, including early prediction models for conditions such as dengue and graft-versus-host disease.
  - Pioneered conformal prediction techniques and causal ML models tailored to health datasets.

- **Biofeedback and Oculometric Research**
  - Developed a biofeedback system that uses eye-tracking metrics to suggest personalized breaks, reducing fatigue during sustained computer use.

- **High-Performance Computing and Containerization**
  - Managed high-performance computing (HPC) systems and implemented Docker containerization, optimizing workflows in data-heavy research.
