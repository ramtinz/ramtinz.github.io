Welcome to my portfolio website! Here I share some of my professional experiences and thoughts.

## Profile

I'm a senior data scientist and research engineer with a PhD in biomedical engineering. Over the past decade, I have contributed to designing and developing several AI systems, e.g. using genetic and clinical data, as well as implementing scalable, efficient software solutions for complex data workflows. I have extensive experience with Python, R, and cloud-based platforms, along with MLOps best practices for deploying and managing machine learning models in production. Additionally, I have developed strong project management and leadership skills, collaborating effectively with cross-functional teams. I am passionate about ideation, prototyping, and driving AI solutions from research to real-world impact.
